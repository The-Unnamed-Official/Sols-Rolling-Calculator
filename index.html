Version V1.25 Update Notes

Updated 2025/10/30

Highlights from the previous release:

  • Rune of Everything no longer triggers glitch visuals outside of the Glitch biome.
  • Runes in general no longer triggers any errors with excluded auras between biomes and runes.
  • Different colors on the update details :D
  • Resetting luck now also clears any Oblivion or Dune preset toggles, preventing stale potion presets from sticking around after adjustments.
  • The version label now opens this changelog so future updates are easier to share.
