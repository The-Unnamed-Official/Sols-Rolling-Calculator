Rotate for the best cutscenes: If possible, rotate your device to landscape before rolling to enjoy cutscenes in their intended view.

Cyberspace notice: Illusionary rolls at a true 1 in 10,000,000 chance in Cyberspace. Your luck value does not affect this roll, so feel free to use any luck level.

Biome requirements: Ensure required events and time settings are active.
