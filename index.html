Note: Cutscenes will now play on every screen size, but it is HEAVILY recommended to use a 16:9 ratio display with a resolution higher than 1280×720 for the best experience.

Rotate for the best cutscenes: If possible, rotate your device to landscape before rolling to enjoy cutscenes in their intended view.

Cyberspace notice: Illusionary rolls at a true 1 in 10,000,000 chance in Cyberspace. Your luck value does not affect this roll, so feel free to use any luck level.

Biome requirements: Ensure required events and time settings are active.

Heads up: Rolling more than 10,000,000 times may take a very long time depending on your computer or device. Continue?

Allow background rolling: Keep processing simulations when this tab is in the background or not focused.

This may increase CPU or battery usage while runs are active. You can disable background rolling anytime from Settings.
